# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Pesantren (Islamic boarding school) staff and teachers — specifically muhafiz (memorization mentors), kepala muhafiz (head mentors/coordinators), and school administrators. They use HalaqahId during class hours on school devices (laptops, tablets, sometimes phones) to manage daily tahfidz operations: recording attendance, logging memorization deposits, tracking student progress across halaqah groups, and reviewing reports.

## Product Purpose

HalaqahId is a tahfidz (Quran memorization) management platform that digitizes the workflow of Islamic schools running halaqah programs. It replaces paper-based tracking of student memorization, attendance, and progress with a structured digital system organized by role:

- **Superadmin**: manages schools and system-wide settings
- **Kepala Muhafidz** (Admin/Koordinator): oversees halaqah groups, muhafiz assignments, and generates reports
- **Muhafiz**: records daily attendance and memorization deposits for their assigned students

Success means teachers spend less time on paperwork and more time mentoring, with full visibility into each student's memorization journey.

## Brand Personality

Professional, serene, focused. The interface should feel like a well-organized classroom — calm, purposeful, and free of visual noise. Teachers are busy; the tool should get out of the way and present data clearly. No urgency, no hype — quiet confidence that the system has everything under control.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboards (cookie-cutter card grids, blue-on-white CRM aesthetics repurposed for education). HalaqahId should feel purpose-built for tahfidz workflows, not a reskinned project management tool.
- Over-engineered admin panels with excessive nested navigation, gratuitous animations, or information overload on every screen.

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity over decoration** — every element earns its place; if it doesn't help a teacher complete a task, it doesn't belong
2. **Calm over urgency** — no visual shouting; the interface respects the contemplative nature of Quran memorization
3. **Purpose-built for tahfidz workflows** — attendance, setoran, and progress tracking are first-class citizens, not afterthoughts
4. **Data-first for busy teachers** — lead with the information teachers need; forms and actions are secondary, not the default view
5. **Respectful of spiritual context without being ornamental** — the product serves Islamic education but expresses that through quality craft, not decorative Islamic patterns or calligraphy

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA compliance. Good contrast ratios, keyboard navigation support, screen reader compatibility. Standard WCAG AA contrast thresholds (4.5:1 for body text, 3:1 for large text). Reduced motion alternatives for any animations. Color is never the sole indicator of state.
